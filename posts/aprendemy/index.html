An easy, selective, accessible and leading e-learning with a commitment in delivering high quality educational services, Aprendemy is a platform that preps high school students for the standardized admission exams, regardless of their location, background, or education level.

This EdTech platform has prepared more than 30k students, with 3 different courses, more than 3000 questions, over 500 video lessons, 15 different exams and statistics on the student progress. More than 2 million questions has been answered on our platform providing in great detail individual student data that has help students to increase their grade on an average 30%.
